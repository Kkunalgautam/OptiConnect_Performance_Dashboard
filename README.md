# OptiConnect_Performance_Dashboard
Performance Dashboard 
Analysis :
During the analyzed period, OptiConnect Solutions handled 1,772 calls, of which 1,311 were successfully resolved, resulting in a 74% resolution rate. While overall operational performance is stable, nearly 26% of calls remain unresolved, indicating opportunities to improve issue closure efficiency.
Call volumes fluctuated throughout the month, with noticeable peaks on specific days, suggesting the need for better workforce planning during high-demand periods. The Television department handled the highest call volume and recorded the highest average talk time (2.24 minutes), indicating more complex customer queries. In contrast, the Air Conditioner department demonstrated higher efficiency with the lowest talk time (2.04 minutes).
Agent performance analysis revealed variation in productivity and customer satisfaction. While some agents managed higher call volumes effectively, satisfaction ratings ranged from 2.75 to 2.93, highlighting inconsistency in service quality. The average call picking time of 55 seconds suggests that response speed may be impacting overall customer experience.
Overall, the analysis indicates that OptiConnect maintains strong operational capacity but can enhance performance by:
Reducing unresolved cases
Improving response time
Standardizing agent performance
Optimizing staffing during peak days
By focusing on these areas, the organization can improve operational efficiency and elevate customer satisfaction levels.
